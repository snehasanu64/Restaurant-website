Est. 2001The Jayanagar Legacy

For over two decades, Donne Biryani House has been a staple of Bangalore's rich culinary landscape. What started as a humble stall in Chickpet has evolved into Jayanagar's favorite destination for authentic, traditional biryani.

Every dish is prepared using secret family recipes passed down through generations. From the slow-cooked mutton to our signature green chili paste, we ensure that every bite takes you back to the roots of Karnataka's flavors.
